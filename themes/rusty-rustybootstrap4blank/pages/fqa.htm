Frequently Asked Questions

If you are a seller you can sign up a seller account using the seller button or just sign up using the buyer button if you have no intention of selling.

The seller account has access to more features relating to selling products and services, i.e You can post products/buy request, make receipt, verify payment and more with a seller account whereas a buyer account can only post buy request, authorize setllement among others. The two types of accounts are made up of only what is neccessary to each account.

Protected Pay secures your payment until you recieve item(s) you paid for. If what you paid for is not delivered to you, you can request a refund. Click here to learn more about how it works.

Direct pay is like every other traditional Online Payment where you can pay directly to trusted sellers for any product you want. Click on Payment options in any of the product/service details page of a product/service you want to buy and then click on direct pay to make payment to the seller of the product/service.

if you have products/services you want to buy but can't find or you need specific features, specification etc., you can post a Buy Request and you will be contacted by sellers that have or supply what you want.

Sellers can post as many products/services as they want.

Only available to seller accounts, Vend credits are used for boosting/promoting posted products/services among other upcoming features.

A Verified Badge attached to a seller's store and posted products/services serves as proof that the account is owned and operated by a specified real individual or organization. This feature is currently only available to registered and known businesses. To request for a verified badge, send an email to support@vend.com.ng.
